04. On Going Projects

August 18, 2021

Simplicity is complex

We’re designing a serene, open-air dining space nestled beside a natural lake in Central Park. The layout emphasizes a seamless blend between architecture and landscape, using reclaimed wood decking, retractable canvas shade structures, and native plant borders. Outdoor seating is arranged to preserve mature trees and optimize lake views. Currently, utilities are being routed and foundation work for the kitchen pavilion is in progress.

September 2, 2024

Design is a process

We’re currently transforming a traditional backyard into a multi-functional outdoor living space featuring a modern paver patio, built-in firepit, custom cedar pergola, and low-maintenance native plantings. The design balances privacy with open flow, using layered greenery and screening trees. Currently in the grading and hardscape phase, with plant installation scheduled for late July.

January 10, 2025

Aesthetic is a decision

This ongoing front yard redesign focuses on xeriscaping principles, replacing water-thirsty grass with a mix of gravel, decomposed granite pathways, and sculptural succulents. A drip irrigation system and solar lighting are being integrated for sustainability and ambiance. We're in the planting phase and already seeing dramatic visual impact.

May 24, 2025

Style is everything

In collaboration with an architect, we’re designing a compact yet elegant courtyard for a brownstone renovation. The space includes linear concrete planters, corten steel edging, vertical green walls, and a calming water feature. Custom furniture and lighting are being fabricated, with installation slated for early August.
